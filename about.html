About IQIS

 Intelligent Quantitative Information Solutions

We are IQIS, a team of experienced data engineers and researchers based in San Francisco. We focus on high quality data and analytics offerings at reasonable pricing, and strive to provide as customized solution to the client as possible.

We have assembled massive and continuously updated data solutions involving weather, real estate, and various economic information for a variety of Fortune 500 firms, from industries ranging from energy, to heavy industry, to food.

A few of our current offerings are described below. If what you are looking for is not here, or if you are looking for something more customized, feel free to get in touch with us to see if we can meet your needs at a competitive price.

Our Data Products

Weather

We provide what we believe is the deepest historical weather coverage available, with global coverage at the precision of a 5 minute walk. Update frequencies can be continuous or scheduled periodic.

Our weather forecast data involves complex in-house statistical modeling. We amalgamate the continuous data streams of over 70 global satellites, feed into to a machine learning optimization framework, and institute a number of data quality protocols. Real-time forecasts extend up to 30 days, and historical forecast coverage extends over 20 years.

Energy

We provide over 20 years of historical and real-time energy price data with coverage for over 90% of the United States, the Eurozone and The United Kingdom. Depending on location, frequency is as granular as prices at each 5 minute interval (24/7). Historical and current information on grid utilization, outages, and generation mix is also available. Price forecasts are available upon request.

Solar

Global solar intensity data from 1965 is available for renewable energy applications, with one-minute frequency data availability beginning in 2012.

Satellite

Micro-level satellite imagery for any latitude and longitude or physical address, from 1985 to today (updated daily), up to 6.5 meter resolution. We also provide object-recognition machine learning analytics services for this data. Please contact us to discuss your needs and pricing.
